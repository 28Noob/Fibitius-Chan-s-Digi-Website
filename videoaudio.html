The Beauty of Speed: A compilation of cool footage and images of a wide variety of different cars, ranging from Miatas to Lamborghinis. Created and edited by Fibitius Chan using OpenShot.

Evaluation

Although this is probably the shortest topic in the 10DGT course, I still found it to be extremely fun. With some previous exprience in some editing softwares such as CapCut, I found it very entertaning to create another edit of some cars and expand my knowledge into other editing softares and in this case; OpenShot. I learnt/expanded some skills such as timing frames correctly, editing videos rather than just photos, using sound effects, adjusting the volume of such sound effects, etc. In terms of expanding these skills even more, I think I can do this by using more transitions, creating longer videos and overall use such editing softwares more often.

I believe that I will use some form of editing software for my final project to potentially create something longer and cooler.
