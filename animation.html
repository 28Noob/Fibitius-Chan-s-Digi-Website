Evaluation

I found this topic to be very educational. What I mean by this is that all these different Blender animations that we have seen online are made like this. A simple repeating process of inserting keyframes and posing. It was very fun to animate as this is my first look at animation. I think that this is one of the easiest forms of animation: using Blender. I learned a lot of skills and techniques to get the right pose on the character and I can extend this by creating longer and cooler animations of characters.

I think that I will definitely be using Blender in my term 3 - 4 project to create simple 3D models or to animate something cool.
